Stir Fry Beef with Green Bean

A stir fry dish made with beef, green beans, and other savory sauces.
I asked my friend who eats this dish religiously why this picture looks like a soup and
how to describe it.

Ingredients

  • 13 ounces of sliced beef
  • 1.5 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1.5 teaspoons of freshly grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons of corn starch
  • 1/2 a cup of beef broth
  • 6 ounces of green beans cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of rice wine
  • 1/4 a teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Combined the 1.5 teaspoons of freshly grated ginger, teaspoon of sugar, tablespoon of soy sauce,
    tablespoon of oyster sauce, and tablespoon of rice wine together in a container.
  2. Place the slices of beef into the container and allow to marinate for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Add cooking oil to a wok and heat until it is hot.
  4. Place the marinated beef and green beans into the wok and cook
    for 2-3 minutes, or until the beef has changed color and the green beans have
    become tender.
  5. Add cornstarch, beef broth, and 2 tablespoons of soy sauce to wok, making sure
    the cornstarch is dissolved.
  6. Wait until the sauce slightly thickens.
  7. When the sauce is slightly thickened, add the sesame oil and mix well before
    removing from heat, then serve.
